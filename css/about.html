Read A Little Bit About Us

Natalie Cisneros is an artist and the designer behind Earthtone Archive, which is based in Richmond, California.

Earthtone Archive was created to share and inspire others to indulge in the romance that the day-to-day can be. Infusing elements of the earth into her work, Natalie hopes to bring to forward the beauty that surrounds us, even while we have our noses stuck in a screen. Whether it be a utilitarian object such as a mug or just an object that brings joy to our shelf, Natalie’s work in ceramics is focused on bringing the outdoors in. While this is a ceramics shop, this is also the artist’s memory. Each piece is influenced by an experience, a place, or a person and is memorialized by her work.

Explore and enjoy her archive of memories.
