How to Care For Your Items

The ceramics shared with you is made from food-safe, durable stoneware clay made in Richmond, CA.

All pieces are dishwasher and microwave safe. Although, it is highly advised to hand wash to ensure extra care and longevity.

Each piece is hand made leaving room for natural warps, dimples, and slight inconsistencies.
